Friends of both Brandon and Karilyn can attest to their obvious fondness for each other. Anyone who spends anytime around them knows that laughter and love abound in their family. Together they look forward to a bright and prosperous future.

Karilyn and her son Jadyn joined Brandon and his children Davion, Malaya, and Amari. Together they welcomed Kymani, and soon after Shayliana arrived to round out their beautiful family. From toddlers to teenagers there is no shortage of youthful energy and joy in their loving home.
